About Me

Hello! I'm Sarah, a passionate artist who finds joy in creating handmade pieces that celebrate the natural world.

I’m inspired by sacred geometry and botanical forms—mandalas, floral lines, and organic symmetry.

My goal is to bring calm, joy, and a touch of nature into everyday life through art and craft.
